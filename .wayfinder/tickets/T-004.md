# T-004: Campaign Engine Specification

`wayfinder:grilling` | Parent: map.md

## Question

Produce the complete specification for PostManager's dispatch scheduling engine. This is the core loop that generates Dispatches from Campaigns, enforces Anti-Detection constraints, resolves Product Variants and template variables, and drives the PostingProvider. Must work for both manual and automated modes.

## Scope

1. **Dispatch generation algorithm** — given a Campaign with P Posts and T Targets, how are P×T Dispatches created? Lazy per-Wave or all upfront? Exact pseudocode.
2. **Scheduler loop** — what runs it? `pg_cron`? Application-level timer? How often? What does each tick do?
3. **Anti-Detection constraint enforcement** — for each constraint (max_posts_per_hour, min/max_delay, cooldown, rotation), exactly how and when is it checked? What happens when a constraint blocks a dispatch?
4. **WA Poster load balancing** — least-busy selection algorithm. How is the running counter maintained? In-memory or in DB? What happens when a WA Poster goes offline mid-campaign?
5. **Product Variant resolution** — sequential vs random cycling. Where is the "last used variant per product × target" tracked?
6. **Template variable resolution** — exact resolution order for `{{Selling Price}}`, `{{WhatsApp Link}}`, `{{Margin}}`, etc. How are calculated variables computed? What happens if a variable can't resolve (missing Product field)?
7. **Dynamic rescheduling** — when a Human Poster is late or misses a dispatch, exactly how do future dispatches shift? Grace period? Max delay threshold? What counts as "missed"?
8. **Wave management** — how are Waves triggered? Are they automatic or manual? How does wave N+1 know wave N is complete?
9. **Campaign status transitions** — what triggers draft→scheduled→running? What conditions cause paused? What triggers completed?
10. **Concurrent safety** — how are race conditions prevented when multiple scheduler ticks fire? Optimistic locking pattern spec.
11. **Campaign editing while running** — how do edits to Posts or Targets propagate to future (not yet generated) Dispatches?
